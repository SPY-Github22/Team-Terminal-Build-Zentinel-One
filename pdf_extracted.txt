Why If-Statements Fail Here
---PAGE BREAK---
Priya (Teller) — downloads 12 records. Never does this. Ever.

The threshold is different for every single person. There are 10,000 employees in a bank. You
cannot write 10,000 different if-statements. That's the exact problem AI solves.
AI doesn't replace your if-statement. It learns what the threshold should be, per person,
automatically.
This is called Anomaly Detection — specifically an algorithm called Isolation Forest (built into
Python's scikit-learn, completely free).
Here's what it does in plain English:
"Look at this employee's last 90 days of behaviour. Learn their pattern. Now look at
today's action. How far is it from their pattern? Score it."
